Our Heritage & Story

A journey of dedication, family tradition, and the art of pure roasting.

Roasting is in Our DNA

Embarking on its journey in Bharuch in 1960, our enterprise represents the pioneering spirit that laid the very bedrock of the peanut and chana industry.

Born from a vision that sought to transform culinary landscapes, we boldly entered the commercial realm by introducing the art of roasting these delectable products. At the heart of our legacy is an unwavering commitment to perfection. We have meticulously crafted a benchmark for quality, where the right roasting meets the highest standards.

Through the decades, we've proudly embraced time-honored methods, ensuring our products not only stand the test of time but also deliver an unrivaled, authentic taste. Today, our history is not just a tale of the past; it's a testament to our dedication to providing an unforgettable journey through flavors that transcend generations.

Methodology

The Roasting Process

We follow a rigorous, touch-free cleaning and slow roasting timeline to lock in flavor and crispness.

1

Raw Material Sourcing

We hand-select premium peanuts, chickpea grains, and grade-A nuts from certified organic farms across Gujarat.

2

Rigorous Cleaning

Multi-stage grading removes smaller kernels and debris. Only plump, uniform ingredients proceed to the next stage.

3

Artisanal Roasting

Sand-roasting in precise temp-controlled ovens to release aromatic oils while preventing moisture retention.

4

Quality Control Check

Continuous testing for taste consistency, moisture percentage, and crispiness parameters.

5

Premium Packaging

Gas-flushed food-grade packaging preserves crispness and aroma. Moisture-impermeable bags seal in the freshness.

6

Distribution

Secure warehousing and expedited shipping reach retailers, global partners, and direct clients intact.
