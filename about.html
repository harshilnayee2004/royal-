Est. 1960

A Snacking Legacy of Sixty Years

Dedicated to the timeless art of roasting premium dry-roasted flavors.

Beat I

The Harvest

We source only the finest raw ingredients directly from Gujarat's organic farms. Every peanut, chickpea, and nut undergoes strict quality selection to ensure optimal flavor development during roasting.

Beat II

The Roast

Our slow-roasting techniques have been passed down through generations. Using controlled traditional methods, we roast our snacks touch-free, enhancing their natural aroma and locking in crispness without artificial additives.

Beat III

The Craft

From paper-thin Premium Pista Chikki to robust Masala Peanuts, every item is crafted with passion. We balance traditional recipes with modern hygienic standards to deliver authentic gourmet snacks.
